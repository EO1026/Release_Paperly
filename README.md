# Paperly

A journaling space where your memories feel alive.


## Why Paperly

Most digital journals feel like documents.  
You open a blank page, type a few lines, and leave.

Paperly is different.

It’s built for people who want their journal to feel:
- visual, not just text
- personal, not generic
- something you want to come back to
- reflective, with AI summaries of your months and years

Write your thoughts, add photos, layer details, and turn everyday moments into pages that actually feel like memories.

## What You Can Do

### ✏️ Write and Compose
Start with text, then build around it.
Add images, links, and collage-style layouts — everything lives naturally on the page.

### 🖼 Shape Your Photos
Edit directly inside your journal:
- remove backgrounds  
- adjust opacity  
- create layered visuals  

No need for external tools.

### 🎞 Add Personality
Use frames, tape, decor, and emoji to give your pages a handcrafted feel — like a digital scrapbook.

### 📄 Customize Your Paper
Choose your background:
- lined  
- grid  
- dotted  

Make it subtle or visible depending on your style.

## Revisit Your Life

Paperly isn’t just about writing — it’s about looking back.

### 📅 Calendar View
See your entries over time and revisit specific days.

### 🔍 Search
Find moments instantly by keywords — people, places, or thoughts.

### 🔄 Looking Back
Reflect on patterns across weeks, months, or years.

### 🤖 AI Q&A
Connect your own API and explore your journal like a conversation.
Ask questions. Discover patterns. Surface memories.

## Your Data, Your Control

- Everything is stored locally on your device  
- Optional biometric protection  
- Export anytime (PDF or JSON)  
- No accounts, no tracking  

Your journal stays yours.


## Built from Real Use

Paperly comes from over a decade of journaling.

It’s not designed for “perfect writing”.  
It’s designed for real life — messy, visual, emotional, and worth remembering.
